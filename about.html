About Pure Petal Stitch

Your trust comes first, and our promises are always delivered on time.

Pure Petal Stitch is a dedicated ladies stitching service based in Gujrat, Punjab. We specialize in creating elegant custom outfits – from daily wear to party wear and traditional dresses – with perfect fitting, neat finishing, and attention to detail.

Our goal is simple: provide high-quality tailoring at affordable prices without compromising on style or trust. We welcome your own designs or help you choose from our styles, with fast 7-day delivery across Pakistan.

Our Promises

  • Neat finishing and proper fitting in every dress
  • Custom designs are always welcome – bring your own or choose from ours
  • Stitched and delivered to your home in just 7 days
  • Affordable rates that fit every budget
  • Friendly, transparent, and customer-first service
